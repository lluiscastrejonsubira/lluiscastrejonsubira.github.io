Lluís Castrejón

I am a PhD student at MILA advised by Aaron Courville. My research is in machine learning, computer vision and natural language processing. I also collaborate with Facebook AI Research Montréal.

I obtained my MSc from the University of Toronto. Before that I spent a year as a visiting student at MIT advised by Antonio Torralba. I obtained B.Sc. degrees in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering from UPC BarcelonaTech, where I collaborated with LARCA.
